French Macarons

Makes 30 Macarons

Ingredients

    Shell:
  • 100 g egg whites
  • 85 g fine sugar
  • 130 g almond flour
  • 130 g confectioners’ sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1–2 drops gel food coloring
    • Filling:
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) butter
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoon water
  • pinch of salt

Directions

    Shell:
  1. Sift the confectioners sugar and almond flour into a bowl.
  2. Add egg whites to bowl and whisk egg whites until foam formed.
  3. Slowly add cream of tartar and fine sugar.
  4. Add food coloring and vanilla.
  5. Fold in 1/3 of dry ingredients.
  6. Add remaining dry ingredients and fold gently.
  7. Move mixture to piping bag with medium round piping tip.
  8. Pipe onto paper and tap to get air bubbles out.
  9. Bake at 300F for 12-15 minutes.
    10. Filling:
  10. Combine sugar and water. Heat until sugar dissolves and bring to boil
  11. Mix egg yolks and whisk until foam
  12. Cook sugar and water until 240F. Remove from heat and slowing add to egg yolks.
  13. Mix egg yolks and sugar and water together.
  14. Slowly add butter
  15. Add vanilla and salt.

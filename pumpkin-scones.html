Pumpkin Scones

Makes 6 scones

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 ½ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice OR:
    • ½ teaspoon allspice
    • ½ teaspoon ginger
    • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
    • ¼ teaspoon cloves
  • 1 ½ teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • ⅓ + cup / 8 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup pumpkin puree
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in bowl.
  3. Slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry while mixing.
  4. Shape into scone
  5. Bake between 12 and 16.5 minutes

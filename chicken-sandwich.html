Chicken Sandwich

Makes 2 sandwiches

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 buns
  • ¼ cup kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon milk powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 cups peanut oil

Directions

  1. Slice chicken breast into 2 equal portions. Combine with 4 cups of water, salt, sugar into ziplock bag. Store in fridge for at least 6 hours.
  2. Combine pepper, paprika, cayenne into bowl
  3. Combine milk and egg.
  4. Combine flour, milk powder, baking powder, pince of salt, pince of sugar, 1 tablespoon of spice mixture
  5. Season chicken breasts in spice, soak in milk/egg, coat with flour
  6. Cook breasts in peanut oil.
  7. Add bun for sandwich.

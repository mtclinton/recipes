Chocolate Chip Cookies

Makes 7 cookies

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons (½ stick) butter
  • ½ cup flour
  • ⅕ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ tablespoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ½ egg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 60 g chopped semisweet chocolate

Directions

  1. Melt butter over medium-high heat for 5 minutes and then transfer to freezer.
  2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt
  3. Whisk sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract together for 5 minutes until mixture falls off whisk in thick ribbons
  4. Add cooled butter and brown sugar to egg mixture.
  5. Add flour to egg mixture and whisk all together
  6. Bake at 325F for 16 minutes
